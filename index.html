About Booking with Artificial Intelligence

Bot09 is here to assist you in finding information about the Monteverde Tourist Offer. You can start by typing 'Hello.' Lito, our tour agent, will be available to help you primarily in English and Spanish, as human intervention is required to complete the tasks. We prioritize security and protection against misuse or potential security breaches. Therefore you will be prompted to provide your email address. This step is essential for us to offer you better support while minimizing risks and ensuring superior customer care.

What is Expected from Bot09

Bot09 is optimized for searching services in Monteverde and nearby areas, focusing on adventure, ecology, and emerging eco/agro-tourism projects. While accommodation and food services are not the main focus, we aim to expand as new partnerships develop. Our goal is to provide up-to-date tourist information and a seamless reservation experience.
