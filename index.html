What is Expected from Bot09

This bot is optimized for searching for services in the Monteverde area and surroundings. The information shown here is related to adventure services, ecology, emerging ecotourism or agroecotourism projects. It is not channeled in the search for accommodation or food services, however as technology grows and new links with merchants develop, this tourist information and reservation site intends to grow accordingly.
